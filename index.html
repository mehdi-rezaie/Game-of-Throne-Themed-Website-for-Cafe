 About Us

The Cafe Of Westeros.

Gospel Of Taste





Cafe

In the North


Started by Samreen Ismail an ardent Game Of Thrones fan, along with her husband Aradhya Kumar, Gospel Of Taste is the first Game Of Thrones-themed cafe in Mumbai. Located on St.Theresa Road, a little ahead of Title Waves, the cafe is already on our must-visit list. The attention to detail at the cafe is impeccable with an Iron Throne, dragon eggs, swords and dragon miniatures adorning the space.

You should be heading here if you’re still reeling from the fact that the last season is two years away, or if you want to claim the Iron Throne.
